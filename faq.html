Frequently Asked Questions

How can we help?

What is the shipping policy?
We offer free shipping on all orders over AED 99. For orders below AED 99, a flat shipping rate of AED 10 applies. Most orders are processed within 1-2 business days.
How do I track my order?
Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can also track your order on our "Orders Tracking" page.
What is your return policy?
We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be in their original condition with tags attached. Please visit our returns page for more information.
Are my payment details secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your payment information. We do not store your credit card details on our servers.
